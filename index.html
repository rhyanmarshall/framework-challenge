New Theme Ideas for Horror Writers

Horror Stories in the Dark

Slide 1: The Introduction

"When the lights go out, the stories begin, each whisper darker than the one before."

Theme:

- set the eerie tone

- dark gradient background

- add a spooky font such as Gothic

- include red accents

- slow fade transition between slides

Slide 2: The Setting

"An abandoned house sits at the edge of town, always radiating mystery and horror."

Theme:

- dark blue or grey background with subtle fog

- use italicized text for body content

- add flicker affect for extra spookiness

Slide 4: The Entrapment

The door slams behind us and in that instant, we realized the house had no intention of letting them leave.

Theme:

- make the viewer feel confined

- black vignette toward the center to create black hole energy

- narrow the width of the content

- overall darker colors and themes
